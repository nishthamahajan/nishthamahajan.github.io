Hello! I am a prospective PhD student looking for positions in the areas of multi-agent systems, game theory and cooperative artificial intelligence.

I was recently a Research Associate at the AI Institute, University of South Carolina where I worked with Prof. Qi Zhang on Intent-Aware Autonomous Driving.

I obtained my MS in Transportation and Infrastructure Systems from Purdue University. Prior to that, I graduated from Indian Institute of Technology Roorkee with a major in Civil Engineering and minor specialization in Computer Science and Engineering.

Email     LinkedIn    

I am interested in studying interactions between autonomous decision-making agents and developing methods by which strategic planning of such interactions can improve the collective performance of a multi-agent system. In particular, I would like to explore how self-interested agents in mixed-motive settings, such as individually-owned self-driving cars sharing a road, can be incentivized to perform better by cooperating and collaborating among themselves, preferably in a decentralized and ad-hoc manner.
